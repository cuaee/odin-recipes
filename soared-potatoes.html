BRAISED POTATOES RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

This is a plate of some nice potatoes, a simple recipe to share with frineds and family.

Ingredients

  • Irish potatoes
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Cameroon pepper
  • Olive oil or plave vegetable oil
  • 2 boiled eggs

    • Steps

    1. Rinse the potatoes with clean water to remove as mush dirt as possible.
    2. Go ahead and peel the outer skin of you potatoes carefully. Rinse carefully afterwards.
    3. Take the peeled potatoes, and cut to your desired measures.
    4. Put in a pot to boil with salt to taste.
    5. let the potatoes boil till it s soft on the inside.
    6. You may want to use a fork to poke holes to get it done faster.
    7. When done, drain the water.
    8. Sprinkle the pepper on it and toss it lightly to mix it all together.
    9. Lightly grease the pan with oil
    10. Put in the potatoes to fry when the oil is hot.
    11. Fry until golden brown.
    12. Put on a paper towel to drain the oil
    13. Serve with your eggs and drink of choice.
