MACARONI SALAD

DESCRIPTION

This is a wonderful and nutrient packed meal. Easy to prepare, difficult to forget. Join us and have a bite!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni pasta
  • Protein (beef, chicken, bacon or whatever you choose)
  • Tomatoes
  • Cooking cream
  • Salt
  • Green Peppers
  • Carrots

    • Steps

    1. Cut up all the vegtables
    2. Sauter them in shallow oil for about 5 minutes.
    3. Boil the macaroni till it is soft enough with a little salt.
    4. Mix the ingredients
    5. Add on a little bit of cooking cream
    6. Serve and enjoy!!
