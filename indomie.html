THE INDOMIE RECIPE

Description

This is a common dish in Nigeria. It is a simple, quick and relatively light meal which can be prepared in just 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • A pack of noodles
  • An onion bulb
  • Fresh pepper
  • Ginger powder
  • Spring onions
  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Fresh tomatoes

    • Steps

    1. cut the onions and slice the tomatoes
    2. Chop up all the veggies
    3. Sauter in a pan with oil
    4. add the sliced tomatoes and ground peppers
    5. add water
    6. let it boil for a while
    7. add the indomie
    8. let cook for 7 minutes
    9. add in the vegetables
    10. let it steam together for 5 minutes
    11. serve or dont - you decide.
